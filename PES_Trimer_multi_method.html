  • Click chips to toggle methods.

  • Click a descriptor button → φ-profile overlay.

Methods. Geometry optimisations and relaxed scan at B3LYP/6-31G, B3LYP/6-31G(d) and B3LYP/6-311+G(d,p) using Gaussian 16. For each method, energies are referenced to opt_1 (ΔE = 0) — the geometry optimised at φ ≈ 176.5°. Descriptor profiles on the right axis are Catmull-Rom interpolations through 7 anchor points per ring per method (opt_1, opt_TS1, opt_2 mirrored across φ = 0° using PES symmetry, plus opt_TS2; filled markers = data, open = mirrored). These overlays are illustrative; quantitative analysis requires per-step descriptor evaluation along the full scan.
