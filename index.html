Methods. B3LYP/6-31G, B3LYP/6-31G(d) and B3LYP/6-311+G(d,p) relaxed scans of D(12,6,7,11) using Gaussian 16. Energies are referenced to opt_1 per method (φ ≈ 176.5°, ΔE = 0). HOMA decomposition (Krygowski): HOMA = 1 − EN − GEO, where EN (« défaut de position moyenne ») measures the mean deviation of the bond lengths from Ropt = 1.3913 Å, and GEO (« défaut d'égalisation des liaisons ») measures the bond-length alternation around the mean. HOMA, EN and GEO are evaluated at all 36 optimised geometries per ring. Six LDM-family descriptors are tabulated at 4 stationary points only; their curves are 7-anchor Catmull–Rom interpolations (3 mirror anchors derived from PES C₂ symmetry about φ = 0°).
